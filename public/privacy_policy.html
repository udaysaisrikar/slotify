Slotify Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details provided when booking or managing appointments.
  • Account Information: Login credentials and preferences.
  • Appointment Details: Services booked, dates, times, location, and service provider information.
  • Usage & Device Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages viewed, and interactions to help us improve the service.
  • Communication Data: Messages, emails, or support requests you send to us.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: See “Cookies & Tracking” below.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain appointment booking and reminders.
  • Send confirmations, updates, invoices, and service notifications.
  • Personalize your experience and improve our features and performance.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with the following parties, only as necessary:

  • Service Providers & Processors: Hosting, analytics, customer support under contracts that protect your data.
  • Business Partners: When you book an appointment with them via Slotify, we share relevant details needed to fulfill the booking.
  • Legal & Compliance: If required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety of users and the public.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to continued protection of your data.

4. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. Despite our efforts, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in and remember preferences.
  • Analyze usage and performance.
  • Personalize content where applicable.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Port your data to another service.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

To make a request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

7. Third-Party Links

Slotify may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

Slotify is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us to delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the “Last updated” date above.
